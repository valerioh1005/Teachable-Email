Maybe you’re starting year three of working from home—or perhaps you’re just now venturing into the lifestyle. Either way, there are a few important things you can do to limit WFH burnout. One of the best ways you can ensure success while working at home is simple: Establish (and stick to) office hours. By confining yourself to certain work hours, you eliminate the issue of work bleeding over into your free time. Although it’s tricky to do when your home is your office and your email is one phone tap away, adhering to firm work boundaries is important for productivity and mental health..
